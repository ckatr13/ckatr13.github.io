About Christina

Christina enjoys performing on Flute, Piccolo, Tin Whistle, Recorder, Clarinet (including Eb and Bass), Saxophone (including Soprano, Alto and Tenor), Oboe, and Piano. She has performed all over the US and Europe, and has performed with artists such as IL Divo, Supertramp, Indigo Girls, Celtic Thunder, Frankie Valli, Video Games Live, Rocktopia and Alan Parsons Project. She regularly performs throughout northeast Ohio with numerous theatre companies, orchestras, and bands. She can be seen performing throughout the US and on national tours. Additionally, she is a music instructor and a web developer.
